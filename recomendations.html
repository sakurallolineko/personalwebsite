Miss monochrome is the first anime I would like to review. It's a great 5 minute anime(the episodes are getting longer! yay!) about a robot, named Miss.Monochrome, who was filthy rich and dreamed about becoming an idol so she could meet her favorite idol, Kikuko, so then Miss.Monochrome's maid tricked Miss.M by telling her that if she gave her maid all of her fortune so she could lead her to becoming an idol. The maid ended up ditching her and so miss.monochrome ended up asking a manager(at a convinience store) if he could be her manager. The anime is still ongoing. The first season aired in 2013 and the second season is airing currently in the summer 2015 season
